Who We Are

Built inside retail. Now fixing it with AI.

Retail AI solutions that improve sales, margin and inventory performance.

BLD.AI is a retail transformation and AI consulting practice that helps retailers solve high-impact commercial problems across inventory, merchandising, space productivity and store execution.

We combine over 20 years of retail operating experience with artificial intelligence, machine learning, automation and data analytics to improve demand forecasting and inventory planning, assortment and stock optimisation, space and planogram productivity, visual merchandising compliance, and store execution and performance visibility.

Unlike technology providers that understand only data, BLD.AI understands how retail decisions are made at head office, in stores and across the supply chain. Our experience spans fashion, FMCG, consumer electronics and lifestyle retail, with exposure to brands including Adidas, Coca-Cola, PepsiCo, GSK, Titan, Timex, BIBA, Tom Tailor, Lavie, Recron, Bira 91 and Stride by Arvind Brands.

BLD.AI builds practical, scalable retail AI solutions designed to reduce revenue leakage, improve margins and enable faster, evidence-based decisions.
